About Securezense

SecureZense security solutions is acclaimed as a growing "True" Value-Added company of passionate consultants in the field of information security with on- the- ground presence in 3 countries. The SecureZense security solutions Company assists clients to identify key risks and define priorities for addressing Information Security gaps and next-generation threat protection. SecureZense security solutions company’s service portfolio focuses on delivering high value security solutions to meet end-to-end security requirements of clients with professional excellence across the region.

What we do?

Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration testing

Our penetration testing goes much further than a typical vulnerability scan, utilising advanced manual techniques to further investigate and eliminate the false positives that are common within an automated scan.

Mobile Application Security Testing

Testing of mobile applications across mobile operating systems such including Android, iOS, Windows and Blackberry, to identify issues with authentication, authorisation, data leakage and session handling.

Security Design and Audit

Reviewing and analyzing the potential risks that later will be prioritized and the possible way of their preventing will be created.

Digital Forensic Investigation

Collecting, analysing and reporting on digital data in a way that is legally admissible. Computer forensics follows a similar process to other forensic disciplines, and faces similar issues.
